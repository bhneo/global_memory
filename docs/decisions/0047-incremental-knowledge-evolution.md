# ADR 0047: Explicit Incremental Knowledge Evolution

- Status: Accepted
- Date: 2026-07-17

## Decision

New material must record `support|refine|limit|contradict|supersede|metadata_only`, previous/new statements, changed fields, reason, trigger source and Evidence added. Acceptance is sequential A→B→C and requires real reuse and update counters.

## Consequences

An unlabeled appended section is not semantic reuse. Both sides of limitations and contradictions remain retrievable.
