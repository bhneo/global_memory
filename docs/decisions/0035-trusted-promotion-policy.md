# ADR 0035: Trusted promotion policy

- Status: accepted
- Date: 2026-07-17

## Decision

Trusted promotion is type-specific and explainable. Claims require atomicity, full supporting evidence and entailment, good extraction, primary authority, applicability, one consolidation pass, and no unresolved contradiction. Concepts require repeated use or independent sources. Analogies preserve both shared structure and break boundary. Frontier objects may remain uncertain but must remain source-linked.

Every decision stores score, passed and failed conditions, sources, contradictions, policy version, actor, and timestamp.
